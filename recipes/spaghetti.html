Spaggheti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients

  • uncooked spaghetti
  • olive oil
  • red pepper flakes, or to taste
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • fresh Italian parsley
  • finely grated Parmigiano-Reggiano chesse

Steps

  1. 1 pound uncooked spaghetti
  2. 1/2 cup olive oil
  3. 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes, or to taste
  4. salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  5. 1/4 cup chopped fresh Italian parsley
  6. 1 cup finely grated Parmigiano-Reggiano chesse
